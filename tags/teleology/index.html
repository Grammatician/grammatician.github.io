This Blog's Premise

 2021-05-06  103 words  Teleology Verb-orientated 
Every field has it’s own unique grammar. These grammars can be noun orientated or verb orientated. Verb orientated grammers are actionable, noun grammars are not, and it is interesting to think about why the Teleology demands a particular grammars orientation. Classical education taught Rhetorical Devices. These are set pieces that can be performed by a writer. However, modernity has all but abandoned this tradition. Linguists merely seek to describe, and language educaters have a distaste for the objective and wallow in the subjective. The purpose of this blog is to develop a new grammar for language, finding a vocabulary that is verb orientated.
